Our Projects

Explore the various projects we have undertaken to promote recycling and sustainability:

Collaborated with Ecogenie

Small Description: Specializes in recycling packaging waste and making products like apparel, blankets, and toys from recycled PET bottles.

Details: This organization maximizes benefits from trash, providing pan-India services and producing sustainable products. Ecogenie helps divert waste from landfills while creating valuable items.

Co-founded Good Food

Small Description: Helps food chains manage waste through tracking, segregation, and recycling.

Details: Good Food collects waste, provides data-driven feedback, and ensures maximum reuse and recycling. Through their app, users track waste metrics. Over 9,000 tons of waste have been diverted.

Partnered with Green Plastic

Small Description: Connects waste-pickers to global markets and promotes recycled plastic for packaging.

Details: Green Plastic provides waste-pickers access to fair market prices and helps brands adopt recycled plastic packaging. It also focuses on reducing ocean plastic by working with coastal communities.
